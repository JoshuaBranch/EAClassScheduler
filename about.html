Senior Life

During my Senior year, I helped create the current club page for the school. The current project I am working on for the school is a student class scheduler. The reason why I decided to create this as my senior capstone project is for multiple reasons. The first reason is because of personal expierence. Building your own schedule is hard. Especially from the many different course options we have here at Explrore Academy. I was spending hours scheduling my classes for different terms and I wanted there to be an easier soultion. The next reason why is I know many peoople don't create their own class schedule which is not a good choice as there are many great choices to choose from. This is the reason why I decided to create this project. I wanted to let students easly create their class schedule with classes they want to take (or need to take) and schedule it with ease.
